Our Services

Have you been struggling with back pain? Get more detailed information on our approach to treating chronic back pain.

Our expert physiotherapists will tailor a rehabilitation plan specific to you and will work with you throughout your rehabilition process to ensure you come back from your injury stronger! Our rates start at $50 a session, schedule a free consultation with one of our physiotherapists to create a rehabilition plan to better estimate the total cost of your therapy.

We offer cryotherapy to give you the best recovery possible. Our cryotherapy sessions are $40 each, there is no need to schedule a consultation appointment prior to your first session and your first session is free!

We offer the best massage therapy in all of Ottawa! Book a consultation with us today!

With the latest technology we are able to place electrodes on the skin and send electrical pulses to different areas of the body. Book a consultation today!
